Eric Daniel Pierre Cantona (French: Éric Daniel Cantona, pronounced [eʁik danjɛl pjɛʁ kɑ̃tɔna]; born 24 May 1966) is a French actor, director, producer, and former professional footballer. He played for Martigues, Marseille, Bordeaux, Montpellier, Nîmes, and Leeds United before ending his career at Manchester United , where he won four Premier League titles in five years and two League and FA Cup Doubles. He won the league championship in seven of his last eight full seasons as a professional. At international level, he played for the French national team.
